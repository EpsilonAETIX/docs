Epsilon HUD

A fully customizable, modern, and high-performance HUD system. Health, armor, hunger, thirst, stress, oxygen, and vehicle indicators.

Version: 2.1.0  |  Resmon: 0.01ms  |  Framework: ESX / QBCore  |  Price: Free

Features

  • Health, armor, hunger, thirst indicators
  • Stress and oxygen bars
  • Vehicle speedometer (km/h or mph)
  • Vehicle fuel gauge
  • Vehicle damage indicator (engine, body)
  • Seatbelt status
  • Minimap customization (circle/square)
  • NUI user settings panel
  • Fully responsive design
  • 0.01ms resmon value
