Epsilon Garage

Advanced garage system - multi-garage support, impound, vehicle sharing, damage logging, and vehicle info panel.

Version: 1.5.0  |  Resmon: 0.00ms  |  Framework: ESX / QBCore  |  Price: Free

Features

  • Multi-garage support (different locations)
  • Vehicle types: car, motorcycle, boat, helicopter
  • Impound (tow) system
  • Vehicle sharing (with other players)
  • Damage and fuel logging
  • Vehicle info panel (plate, model, damage)
  • Garage capacity limit
  • Modern NUI interface
